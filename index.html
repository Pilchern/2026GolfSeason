Standards of Play

Season Rules

Near-official PGA-style rules, real scores posted to GHIN, no cutting corners.

Scoring & Handicaps
  • All scores posted to GHIN after every competitive round
  • Handicap Index maintained through the USGA GHIN app
  • Gross score determines event winner unless otherwise noted
  • Net score side bets are optional and event-specific
  • Pre-round handicap verification before each event
On-Course Standards
  • Putt everything out — no gimmies during official events
  • Play the ball down (lift, clean, and place only if course mandates)
  • No soft concessions in stroke play
  • USGA Rules of Golf apply for all rulings and drops
  • Scorecards signed and attested after each round
Major Championships
  • Three majors carry the heaviest point values (250, 300, 400)
  • Strictest conditions: no gimmies, no concessions, ball played down
  • Final Major at 400 points can swing the entire season
  • Optional expanded format for First Major
Signature Events
  • Bittersweet: No quitting under any circumstances
  • Quitting at Bittersweet = automatic event loss + optional 3-hole deduction
  • All putts holed out at signature events
  • Chalet Hills: front 9 match play, back 9 stroke play
